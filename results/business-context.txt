=== BUSINESS PROBLEM ===

Construction cost estimation in Ukraine requires generating detailed technical documents that comply with national building standards (DBN — Державні будівельні норми, DSTU — Державні стандарти України). These documents include:
- Bills of Quantities (BOQs) with material specifications and pricing
- Cost estimate summaries (кошторисна документація)
- Resource statements with labor, materials, and equipment breakdowns
- Technical specifications referencing current standards and norms

Currently, creating these documents is manual, time-consuming, and error-prone. Each document requires:
- Knowledge of current material prices across regions
- Understanding of applicable building standards and norms
- Correct application of pricing coefficients and overhead calculations
- Proper formatting according to construction documentation standards

ALD's ERP system processes thousands of such documents annually. The company has accumulated 6 years of structured procurement data covering the entire lifecycle of cost estimation.

=== WHY A NEW GENERATIVE AI MODEL IS IMPERATIVE ===

1. DOMAIN-SPECIFIC LANGUAGE: Construction cost estimation uses highly specialized terminology, codes, and formats that general-purpose LLMs do not understand. Ukrainian construction norms (DBN, DSTU) are not represented in public training corpora.

2. STRUCTURED OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS: Cost estimation documents must contain precise numerical calculations, material quantities, unit prices, and totals that are internally consistent. General-purpose models cannot reliably generate such structured, computation-dependent documents.

3. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE: Documents must strictly comply with Ukrainian building standards. Incorrect references to standards or wrong pricing coefficients can invalidate entire estimates. No existing model has this knowledge.

4. PROPRIETARY DATA: ALD's 6-year dataset of real procurement transactions, supplier prices, regional cost variations, and project outcomes represents unique domain knowledge. Training a custom model on this data will produce capabilities unavailable from any public model.

5. REAL-TIME PRICING INTEGRATION: The model must incorporate current market prices from ALD's ERP database. This requires custom architecture that can condition generation on structured database inputs, not just text prompts.

=== TRAINING DATA ===

Data preparation pipeline:
- Extract structured records from ERP database

- Validate and clean data for model training
- Estimated training corpus: multi-GB after conversion

=== PROPOSED MODEL ARCHITECTURE ===

CosTERRA will be based on a transformer architecture, fine-tuned/trained specifically for construction cost estimation document generation:

- Base: Open-source foundation model (e.g., Llama, Mistral, or domain-appropriate multilingual model)
- Fine-tuning approach: Supervised fine-tuning on instruction-output pairs derived from ALD's historical data
- Custom components: Structured data encoder for integrating ERP database inputs (prices, materials, standards)
- Output format: Structured cost estimation documents compliant with Ukrainian building standards
- Model size: Multi-billion parameters (justifying HPC resources beyond playground access)
- Training: Full fine-tuning on EuroHPC pre-exascale GPU clusters (thousands of GPU-hours)
